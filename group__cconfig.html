◆ cFLASH_MODE

enum cFLASH_MODE

Strategy of writing blocks (new or modified blocks).

Enumerator
CDUMMY_MODE 

Free blocks are ignored. Compressed block is written at the end of the storage file (archive). Fastest but not space efficient.

COPTIMIZED_MODE 

Space saving strategy. Compressed block is written instead of free block with minimum suitable size. Size of such free block's size is larger or equal to compressed block's size. Slowest one.

CFIRST_SUITABLE_MODE 

A strategy between CDUMMY_MODE and COPTIMIZED_MODE. Compressed block is written instead of first found free block in storage file with larger or equal size.
